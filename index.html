Description: This project was developed for pople who live in shared apartments or shop for groceries in the groups. The main goal of the project is to distribute the store bill and items among the shoppers. The project is run as follows: Begin by photographing the store receipt and uploading it to the click-n-split app. A page with email address and name input fields appears. All shoppers associated with the bill should enter their names and email addresses. All of the items from the receipt will be displayed in a data-table format on the dashboard, along with their price, quantity, and checkboxes. By selecting their names from the dropdown menu, users can check the checkboxes for their own items.
